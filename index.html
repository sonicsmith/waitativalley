Waitati Valley Equestrian Centre

Equestrian centre open for hire. Waitati Valley Equestrian Centre has a newly built all-weather (sand and rubber, full drainage) full-sized out door dressage arena, stables /stalls, individual lockable feed and tack sheds and grazing for hire. There is also a small BBQ area, bathroom facilities and laundry. We welcome you to Waitati Valley Equestrian Centre.

Services

Visitors:
For Arena use have to book prior with Mark (0272087487). $10 per hour Arena. $15 per hour Arena and Jumps. Overnight Grazing $10 per horse (subject to availability) Free day parking.

Regulars:
Do not need to book prior. $10 per hour Arena. $15 per hour Arena and Jumps. Overnight Grazing $10 per horse (subject to availability) Free day parking. Yearly subscription $100.
Residents: Arena and jumps unlimited use $25 per week per person Grazing $25 per week per horse Stable and lockable tack room $25 per week Unlimited Free parking
