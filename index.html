Waitati Valley Equestrian Centre

Equestrian centre open for hire. Waitati Valley Equestrian Centre has a newly built all-weather (sand and rubber, full drainage) full-sized out door dressage arena, stables /stalls, individual lockable feed and tack sheds and grazing for hire.
