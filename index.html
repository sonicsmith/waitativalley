Waitati Valley Equestrian Centre

Equestrian centre open for hire. Waitati Valley Equestrian Centre has a newly built all-weather (sand and rubber, full drainage) full-sized out door dressage arena, stables /stalls, individual lockable feed and tack sheds and grazing for hire. There is also a small BBQ area, bathroom facilities and laundry. We welcome you to Waitati Valley Equestrian Centre.
